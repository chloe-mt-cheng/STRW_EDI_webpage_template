Peanut Chaat

Vegetarian | Preparation time: 15-20 minutes

Ingredients

    Spiced peanuts* (​Katjang Pedis​) (150-200gm)

    2 medium-sized onions

    1 large tomato or ~5 cherry tomatoes

    2 carrots

    1 large lemon

    Coriander leaves

    Mint leaves

    1 tsp pepper powder

Preparation

  • Grate the carrots (if you don’t have a grater, cut the carrots into really tiny pieces)
  • Dice the onions and tomato. Roughly chop the mint and coriander leaves
  • Add all the ingredients into a big bowl, and add some lemon juice. (Be generous with the lemon juice, it is the only dressing!)
  • Add the peanuts, pepper and mix it all up.
  • For those who want to make it more Indian, add some ​chaat masala​ and/or green chillies (if you dare ;))
>

Tips

Tip 1

The longer you wait before eating it, the soggier the crunchy peanuts get. So keep the portion size in mind while mixing everything.


Tip 2

If you want to store it to eat later, do so before adding the peanuts.
