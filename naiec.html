The Netherlands Astronomy Equity and Inclusion Committee (NAIEC)


The Netherlands Astronomy Equity and Inclusion Committee (NAIEC) is a body that advises the Netherlands Astronomy Council and is available for guidance and advice for Equity, Diversity, and Inclusion in Dutch astronomy. The NAIEC consists of astronomers and astronomy engineers at all astronomical institutes in the Netherlands. It is chaired by Samaya Nissanke (on leave, temporarily chaired by Betsey Adams). The goal of the NAIEC is to foster a fairer and more inclusive astronomical community in the Netherlands.
